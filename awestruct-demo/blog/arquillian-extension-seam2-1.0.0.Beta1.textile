We have bunch of small but important improvements in our extension for all Seam 2 users out there.

h3. Some of the highlights in this release

<i class="icon-star"></i> Injection of @Identity@ is now supported in the Arquillian powered tests.

<i class="icon-star"></i> Your tests can now exercise conversation scoped components!

<i class="icon-star"></i> Seam 2.3 and JBoss AS 7 are now covered by our test suite thanks to "Thiago Veronese":https://github.com/tveronese

<i class="icon-star"></i> Seam 2 autopacking based on Maven can now use arbitraty @settings.xml@ when specified through @mvn.alternate.settings@ system property.

We look forward to hearing your feedback about this release in the "community forums":#{site.project_space}!
